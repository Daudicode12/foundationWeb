Welcome To Foundation Of Christ Church

Foundation Of Christ Miracles Church is a domenion that targets to convert and teach none Christians and None-believers to know about God's salvation, and making the relationship between human and the Most High God. The Church also focuses on nourishing the spiritual and mental growth

Bishop Francis Odipo

Foundation Of Christ Church Was started by Bishop Francis Odipo Otieno. He accepted God's calling to preach His Gospel to different areas and alo targeting to convert may people to start believing in the Lord

The Church was founded on 12-05-2004 The foundation of this church was set first in Ongata Rongai area in Kajiado County

Churches

Foundation Of Christ Miracles Church have various branches across Kenya within different counties, these branches have God chosen Servants Who have realy sacrificed to do The Lord's work
